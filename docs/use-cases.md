# TPSReport use cases

**[TPSReport](https://tpsreport.pro)** supports many knowledge workflows. This Obsidian plugin is the fastest way to get local research into that system.

→ [What is TPSReport?](about-tpsreport.md) · [FAQ](faq.md) · [tpsreport.pro](https://tpsreport.pro)

---

## Research OS for teams

Curate findings in Obsidian, publish as structured [TPSReport](https://tpsreport.pro) reports, and maintain graph-linked research collections.

- Link related studies with frontmatter (`entities`, `topics`, `keywords`)  
- Track when key notes go stale via KnowOps in TPSReport  
- Enable Graph RAG for agent-assisted literature review  

**Workflow:** Obsidian folder → Publish as New Report → push updates → query in [TPSReport](https://tpsreport.pro)

---

## Internal knowledge wikis

Replace flat wiki hierarchies with **category-tagged, relationship-aware** knowledge.

- HR, Engineering, Legal — scoped collections with filtered retrieval  
- Hub documents surfaced by centrality scoring  
- Pull cloud edits back into Obsidian for local editing  

---

## Compliance & policy management

Build regulatory knowledge graphs: policy → procedure → audit trail.

- Explicit supersession and version relationships in report structure  
- Freshness alerts when regulations change  
- Controlled visibility per destination in [TPSReport](https://tpsreport.pro)  

---

## Technical documentation

Sync API docs, runbooks, and troubleshooting guides from Obsidian.

- Prerequisite relationships via linked notes and metadata  
- Image sync for diagrams embedded in Markdown  
- Conflict copies when local and cloud edits diverge  

---

## Product strategy & decision logs

Connect customer insights, competitive research, and product decisions.

- `intents` and `scenarios` frontmatter for agent routing  
- Trace reasoning chains across published reports  
- RAG-indexed collections for strategy copilots  

---

## Customer success knowledge bases

Curate support articles with category tags and usage-aware maintenance.

- Billing, Integrations, Troubleshooting — taxonomy-driven retrieval  
- Push updated articles from Obsidian after ticket learnings  
- Monitor stale articles in [TPSReport](https://tpsreport.pro)  

---

## Personal knowledge → team knowledge

Start in a private Obsidian vault folder; publish to team or org destinations when ready.

- Visibility and edit-scope controls at publish time  
- Stable `node_id` identity across push/pull cycles  
- Optional RAG indexing for team-wide search
